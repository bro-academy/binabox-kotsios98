The Basilisks NFT

Gaming
Tony Nguyen 20 June 2022

Basilisks aren’t just creatures — they’re digital apex predators. Forged on the Ethereum chain and raised in a metaverse colosseum, they live to dominate, duel, and devour. Every one of them is an NFT with unique DNA, traits, and bloodlust.

  • In this game, your Basilisk is your warrior. You breed it. You name it. You train it. And when you’re ready, you send it into the arena — not to play, but to survive.
  • Each NFT Basilisk has a set of traits, mutations, and skills, minted immutably on-chain. The arena? A fully 3D metaverse battleground with wagers, livestreams, and faction wars.
  • The game’s AI learns from every move. If your Basilisk gets too strong — it becomes a target. If it dies? It's burned forever. If it wins? You earn crypto, glory, and power.
  • Unique DNA structure
  • PvP-enabled arenas
  • Permanent death mode
  • Metaverse integration
